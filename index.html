Home | Karle Flanagan

I graduated in May of 2012 with a degree in Mathematics from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I minored in Statistics and Secondary Education and obtained an Illinois State teaching certificate for 6th-12th grade mathematics.

In August of 2012, I started the Statistics M.S. program at the University of Illinois and graduated in December 2013.

I currently teach introductory statistics (Stat 100 and 200) at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I love helping students from many different majors gain a new appreciation for statistics and data science!

I have experience teaching at the middle school, high school, and college level. I also have experience in the insurance industry using SAS and predictive analytics.
